Itens Homebrews Adicionados

Anteriormente não existiam todos os itens que aumentavam todos os atributos para 19. Então modificamos o de destreza e criamos os seguintes itens abaixo:

Ring of Dexterity - Raro Maior

  • Your Dexterity score is 19 while you wear these ring. They have no effect on you if your Dexterity is already 19 or higher without he.

Periapt of Wisdom - Raro Maior

  • Your Wisdom score is 19 while you wear these periapt. They have no effect on you if your Wisdom is already 19 or higher without he.

Necklace of Charisma - Raro Maior

  • Your Charisma score is 19 while you wear these necklace. They have no effect on you if your Charisma is already 19 or higher without he.
